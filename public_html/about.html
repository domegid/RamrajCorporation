About Us

We are one of the most proficient Manufacturers, Importers, Traders and Suppliers of Fuel Oil, Carbon Black, Tyre Steel Wire, Packaging Items (Plastic/Steel/Wooden), Rubber Scraps and allied products, Plastic Scrap, PPGI/HR/CR Coil etc.
